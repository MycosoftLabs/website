# CREP User Persona Test: U.S. Marine Corps Commander

## Persona Profile

**Role:** U.S. Marine Corps Expeditionary Force Commander  
**Clearance:** SECRET / TOP SECRET  
**Primary Mission:** Amphibious operations, expeditionary warfare, crisis response  
**Key Requirements:** Coastal/beachhead assessment, environmental hazards, rapid deployment planning, biological threat awareness

## Use Cases & Requirements

### Primary Use Cases
1. **Amphibious Landing Assessment** - Evaluate coastal environmental conditions
2. **Expeditionary Operations Planning** - Assess environmental threats in deployment zones
3. **Wildfire Impact on Operations** - Monitor fires affecting ground operations
4. **Biological Threat Assessment** - Identify biological hazards in operational areas
5. **Rapid Response Planning** - Quick environmental assessment for crisis response
